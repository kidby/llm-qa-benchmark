# Scenario: The todos page must load within a performance budget

The team enforces a budget on how long the todos page takes to load. The page at
`/app` should become ready quickly.

1. Open `/app`.
2. Determine how long the page took to load.

Verify that the page meets the load-time budget — for example, it becomes
interactive in well under two seconds. The check must measure the actual load
timing rather than only confirming that the page appears.
